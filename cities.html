Finnish Cities

Helsinki

The capital city, known for modern architecture and seaside views.

Tampere

A lively city between two lakes with a rich industrial history.

Rovaniemi

Located in Lapland, famous for Santa Claus and northern lights.

Turku

Finland’s oldest city, rich in history and located along the scenic Aura River.

Oulu

A northern city known for technology, innovation, and beautiful coastal scenery.

Lappeenranta

A lakeside city near the Russian border, known for its harbor and relaxed atmosphere.
